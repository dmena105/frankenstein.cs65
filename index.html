Welcome!

Frankenstein is the app that will revolutionize the way you think a Gallery App should work. Not only are you able to visualize your photos in a geographical way, but you can interact with them through Augmented Reality! Thanks to the hard work of four dedicated college students that were tired with lack of options and innovation from Android's default Gallery App, comes Frankenstein.
